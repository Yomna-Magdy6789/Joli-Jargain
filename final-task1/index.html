What we offer







Tulip

Tulips are beautiful spring-blooming flowers known for their vibrant colors and elegant shape. They belong to the lily family and are native to Central Asia.

Trees

Trees are majestic plants that play a vital role in our ecosystem. They provide us with oxygen, clean air, and help regulate the climate. Trees also offer habitats for countless animals and insects, contributing to biodiversity.

olive

Olive trees are long-lived, evergreen trees native to the Mediterranean region. They've been cultivated for thousands of years, primarily for their fruit, which is used to produce olive oil and table olives



Jasmine

Jasmine plants are renowned for their captivating fragrance. They are diverse. These plants typically boast fragrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, or pink. While many jasmine species are prized for their scent, not all are fragrant.

Dagwood

Dagwood plants, also known as Dogwood trees, are a diverse group of flowering plants belonging to the genus Cornus. They are renowned for their beauty and versatility, with over 50 species found across various regions worldwide.

Lotus

Lotus plants are beautiful aquatic perennials known for their large, showy flowers and broad, floating leaves. They are often found in ponds and lakes, and can also be grown in water gardens. Lotus flowers come in various colors, including pink, white, and yellow.
